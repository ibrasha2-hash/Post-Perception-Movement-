## **Are You Prepared to Face Questions We Do Not Have the Luxury of Answering?**

Post-Perception is not a collection of ready-made answers. If it were, it would be merely another ideology demanding your faith. 

This movement is a **permanent laboratory**. 

No work within it tells you anything. Instead, each work **interrogates your certainty**—your senses, your trust in what you see and touch, and your relationship with time, authority, and meaning.

---

## **What Is Post-Perception?**

In an era where truths dance upon screens of deception, and where digital images become more real than reality itself, **Post-Perception** deconstructs the relationship between perception and reality.

### The Core Crisis
- 96% of new digital content is produced by AI (2020)
- Deepfakes are indistinguishable from reality (2023–2025)
- Algorithms shape what we see without our awareness
- Our senses have become an unreliable guide to truth

### Our Response
**Art as a perceptual laboratory for cultivating perceptual immunity.**

Instead of answering questions, we pose them:
- *Does what we see truly exist?*
- *Can our senses be trusted?*
- *What happens when perception becomes fragile?*

---

## **The Nine Principles**

1. **Perception is Fragile** — The senses are not reliable; they can be manipulated
2. **Truth is Constructed** — No absolute, fixed truth exists
3. **Language is Power** — Words exercise authority over our perception
4. **Art is a Laboratory** — Art tests, disrupts, and retrains perception
5. **The Recipient as Partner** — The viewer is incomplete without the artwork
6. **Perceptual Shock as Gateway** — Contradiction disrupts automaticity
7. **Destruction as Creation** — Deconstruction liberates new meaning
8. **Art as Perceptual Immunity** — Art trains consciousness to resist deception
9. **Continuous Experimentation** — The movement remains open to evolution
